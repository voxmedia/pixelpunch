    Join Vox Media on Thursday, October 23rd at 7pm for a fun night of pixel pushing at its finest.

    Five brave designers will compete head to head with timed challenges that test their creativity and ability to think on their feet. Pixel Punch is the first time the design community will get a look inside the wild, zany, business-serious world of making things at Vox Media. Were excited to bring together new friends to share ideas. Were pretty confident in our ability to provide a fun environment and your promise to bring mad skills. You promise, right?

    Contenders

    Victor Ware

    Victor Ware is a web designer and native Washingtonian. He became a designer on a whim in college and quickly fell in love with it. In his spare time he enjoys producing music and volunteering. He is currently designing at AOL.

    @NVRSLPS

    Josh Mahan

    A recent graduate from American University, Josh now works for IMGE, a local ad agency. There, he responds to a lot of emails and sometimes makes stuff. Josh enjoys good design and good coffee, and frequently writes about himself in the third person.

    @squashmahan

    Joseph Le

    Joseph joined Viget after graduating from George Mason University with a B.F.A in Graphic Design. Born and raised in Virginia, he enjoys spontaneous adventures, chilling with friends, and perfecting his break-fu skills (a combination of breakdancing and kung-fu).

    @joseph_le

    Danielle Theroux

    D is a design darling currently mixing things up at an ad agency next door to the White House. She's pretty keen on branding, user experience and storytelling. Her maxim? Pressure makes diamonds.

    @ladydeetee

    Peter Verastegui

    Originally from Bolivia, I like to work with colorful palettes and bold graphics. My work strives for a cohesive and branded aesthetic with a hint of bright colors, I enjoy the process that precedes the final design, things like sketching and type iterations.

    @petveras

    Judges

    @carolynsewell

    Jason Santa Maria

    Terry Biddle

    Who We Are

    Vox Media is one of the country's largest and fastest growing online publishers. Based in Washington DC, Vox owns and operates sites in distinct vertical categories: general news, sports, technology and culture, gaming, dining and nightlife, shopping and fashion, and design and real estate.

    The Vox Media product team is made up of designers, developers, operations engineers, and product and community managers, based in Washington, DC, New York, and Austin, and distributed remotely in cities from Santa Barbara, California to Springfield, Missouri.
